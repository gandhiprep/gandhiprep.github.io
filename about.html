Jai Gandhi


Jai is a senior at Metea Valley High School in Aurora, IL. Outside of tutoring, Jai loves to play tennis and basketball. He is very involved in the business and financial world and is also the Director of Finance at charity Meaningful Summers. In addition to this position, he is involved in two other charities, and loves to help the community in any way he can. Jai loves reading the news, being outdoors, and going on adventures. In school, he is the President of his Business Professionals of America chapter, and the CEO of his Virtual Enterprises International firm. In the future, he wants to work in the financial sector or run his own business.


After scoring a perfect 36 on his first try at the ACT in 2017, dozens of friends came to him complaining about their current tutors and asking for help. To fill this need, Jai created GandhiPrep - which began as an ACT tutoring service. Starting in early 2018, Jai has helped others reach their goals and improve their standardized test scores. Jai went through many different tutoring courses himself, combining their own components, as well as adding his own flair, to create GandhiPrep. GandhiPrep has since achieved major success, raising the average ACT scores of its students by 4 points. Since then, he has expanded his business to include other tests and classes, to help as many people as he can.
