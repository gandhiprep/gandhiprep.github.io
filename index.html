GandhiPrep Tutoring

Jai Gandhi - ACT Tutor

After scoring a perfect 36 on his first try at the ACT, dozens of friends came to him complaining about their current tutors and asking for help. To fill this need, Jai created GandhiPrep - a personalized ACT tutoring service. Started in early 2018, Jai has helped others reach their goals and improve their standardized test scores. Jai went through many different tutoring courses himself, combining their own components, as well as adding his own flair, to create GandhiPrep.
